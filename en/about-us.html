Our beginnings

We emerged from the union of software and financial tech teams who have been working on various transactional solutions since 2005. In 2007, they decided to collaborate formally, creating a company named Orugga.

Orugga primarily focused on developing mobile transactional solutions, including both micro and macro payment systems. With proven expertise in creating high-end mobile entertainment, ticketing, and rich media content solutions, the team added value-added products and services for network operators. The Orugga team has extensive experience in the mobile and financial markets, developing diverse solutions for leading brands.

In November 2008, Orugga received capitalization from a private investors to expand the company’s global reach.

Our Mission

At Orugga Mobile Innovation, our mission is to transform mobile and financial technology into innovative transactional solutions that empower our clients’ growth. We specialize in creating payment systems, mobile entertainment, ticketing and rich content, offering high value-added products and services for network operators and leading brands.

What drives us?

We are driven by the belief in uniting talent, experience, and vision to redefine how people and companies interact with technology, creating an impact in both local and global markets.
